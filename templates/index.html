Quick Note on chapter headings

I use a hashtag system to try and indicate to myself and others the status of a particular chapter. For example #inprogress means I'm working on it at the moment so it'll be a nasty mixture of prose and bullet-pointed notes. The tags will usually be intuitively named, but just in case here are the usual suspects:

  • #notes means that the chapter is basically just a brain dump of initial thoughts and shouldn't be read too carefully, or at least critiqued at a conceptual level
  • #inprogress means I'm working on it at the moment. It'll be somewhere between #notes and #draft
  • #draft means I've drafted the vast majority of the chapter and I'm expecting more in-depth feedback to result in some changes, but the flow and the content is pretty much there.
